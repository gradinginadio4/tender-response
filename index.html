Tender Response Time

An Observation
In competitive tenders for hospitality equipment, response speed often creates advantage. Companies that respond in 48-72 hours frequently capture attention before slower competitors complete internal processes.
Typical response time 10-14 days
Fast competitor response 48-72 hours
Common internal delays Technical validation, management approval, finance sign-off

The goal isn't reckless speed—it's removing unnecessary friction. Some companies have maintained quality while accelerating through pre-approved frameworks and parallel processes.

How Some Companies Accelerated

Brussels-based distributor, 90 employees
Created pre-approved margin bands (15-25%) allowing sales directors to sign quotes up to €250K without escalation. Only exceptions go to management. Eliminated 3-day wait on 70% of tenders.
Antwerp-based distributor, 70 employees
Developed standard configuration templates covering 80% of typical hotel and restaurant setups. Engineers review only exceptions, not every quote. Technical validation dropped from 3 days to 4 hours.
Ghent-based integrator, 60 employees
Shifted from sequential review (sales → technical → finance → management) to parallel process using shared dashboard. All departments review simultaneously. Total time reduced 60% without quality compromise.
